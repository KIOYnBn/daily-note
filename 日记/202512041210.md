# 翻译
* Since ancient time, China has had a significant imbalandce in its water resources, with scarcity in the north and abundance in the south. 
* To effectively address the severe water resources shortage problem in the north, the Chinese government implemented the South-to-North Water Diversion Project.
* This is a grand water resource allocation project across regions.
* After decades of planning and preparation,  the project began construction in 2002 and is divided into three routes: east, middle, and west.
* The total length is 4350 kilometers, benefiting over more 400 million people.
* Since the water was put into use in 2014, the project has transferred more than 500 billion cubic meters of water to the north, providing reliable water resources for the people in the north and significantly improving the ecological environment of this area, promoting the sustainable and healthy development of the economy
