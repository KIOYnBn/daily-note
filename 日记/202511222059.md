* ambitious：
	* 有野心的
		* They were ambitious for their children
	* 耗时的
		* the government's ambitious plans for social reform
* deficiency
	* 缺乏
		* Vitamin deficiency in the diet can cause illness
	* 缺点
		* deficiencies in the computer system
* intrinsically: 本质地
	* Psychologists also agree curiosity is intrinsically motivated
* measure
	* v
		* 测量
			* A ship's speed is measured in knots
		* 短语
			* measure sth out: 取出
				* He measured out a cup of milk and added it to the mixture
	* n
		* 措施， 方法
			* safety/security/austerity measures
		* 度量单位
			* The Richter Scale is a measure of ground motion

			* an accurate measure of ability
		* 习语
			* beyond measure及其， 非常
				* He irritated me beyond measure

			* full/short measure足量/不足量
				* We experienced the full measure of their hospitality
			* in full measure完全
* navigate
	* v
		* 导航
			* to navigate by the stars
		* 航行
			* The river became too narrow and shallow to navigate
		* 正确处理
			* We next had to navigate a complex network of committees
		* 浏览
			* Lots of these sites are hard to navigate through
* smooth
	* adj
		* 平滑的
			* the smooth surface of the metal
		* 顺利的
			* They are introducing new measures to ensure the smooth running of the business

			* The car's improved suspension gives you a smoother ride
		* 圆通的
			* I don't like him. He 's far to smooth for me
		* 味道醇和的
			* This coffee has a smooth, rich taste
	* v
		* 使平滑
			* He smoothed his hair back
		* 克服困难
			* She spoke to both sides in the dispute in an attempt to smooth over
