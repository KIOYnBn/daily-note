# 选词填空
* offset: 抵消， 补偿
	* offset sth
		* Prices have risen in order to offset the increased cost of materials
	* offset sth against sth
		* Wht expens cn you offset against tax
* escape
	* V
		* 逃脱
			* Two prisoners have escaped
		* 被忘掉
			* Her name escapes me
	* n
		* 解脱， 消遣
			* Craig finds escape in dreams
* perpetually: 永恒地
	* They were all perpetually starving
* pile
	* n
		* 成堆的东西
			* a pile of clothes
		* 习语
			* at the bottom/top of the pile处于无足轻重或举足轻重的地位
			* make a/your pile赚钱
	* v
		* 堆放， 积累
			* Snow was piled up against the door
		* 习语
			* pile on the agony/gloom雪上加霜
				* Bosses piled on the agony with threats of more job loses
			* pile on 剧增
				* I've been piling on the pounds
			* 夸大其词
				* Don't pile on the drama
			* 增加工作
				* He felt his boss was piling too much work on him
			* pile up堆积
				* Work always piles up at the end of the year
