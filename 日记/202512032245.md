# 翻译
Zhang Qian was the China's first great explorer. Braving hardships and overcoming difficulties, he made two diplomatic missions to Western Regions. He opened up the trade relations between China and West Asia and Europe, shipped the Chinese silk and silk fabrics to West Asia and Europe, and opened up the famous "Silk Road" in history.
At the same time, he introduced the West Regions' customs, geographic culture and unique species to the Central Plains, Which greatly broadened people's horizons.
As the historians have pointed out, the opening of Silk Road would not have been possible, and there would not have been cultural exchanges bewteen Han dynasty and West Regions or Europe.
