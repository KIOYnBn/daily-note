* you parents also followed a partially vegetarian diet

* Thanks for inviting me out tonight
	* 句意
	* tonight: 今晚
* I've been wanting to try this place for weeks
	* 句意
* I've been dying to come here
	* die: 渴望
* the menu might not be accomodating
	* accomodate:  迎合
* I can't imagine giving up a premium juice steak or bacon cheese burger
	* premium: 优质的
	* juice: 饮料
	* steak： 肉排
	* bacon： 胚根
	* cheese： 奶酪
	* burger： 汉堡
* I never really had a craving to it
	* crave: 渴望
* I bet it would be hard enough, giving up something you're accustomed to eating
	* bet: 打赌
	* accustomed: 习惯的
* I tried cutting back coffee in the morning
	* cut back: 减少
