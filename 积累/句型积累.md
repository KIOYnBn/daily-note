
- 定语从句
	- The jobs reports that the Bureau of Labor Statistics releases every month are an exception
		- 正常语序： the Bureau of Labor Statistics releases that every month
- No matter how + **形容词/副词** + **主语** + **系动词/谓语动词**
	* example
		* no matter how reasonable our efforts to decrease dependency
	* 当 **no matter how**（以及类似的 however， whatever， whether...or...）引导让步状语从句时，如果从句的谓语动词是 **be**，且主语和谓语是**主系表**结构，常常可以省略动词 **be**，甚至有时连同主语一起省略。
* 倒装句
	* **表语 + 系动词 + 主语**
		* More effective, perhaps, solutions that fill the empty space
			* 省略了be动词
	* `类比+倒装`
		- Budgets are shrinking, as is the size of the agency
- thousands of times as broad as it is deep
- but it's not as if the expedition has been a failure.
	- as if引导的从句当表语
