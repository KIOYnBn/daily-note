## 突然想到
- It occurs to sb ....
	It occurs to sb that + 从句某人突然想到
	It occurs to sb to do sth某人突然想到去做某事
## 事件的原因
- attribute sth to sth： 把...归因于 ....
	The rapid growth is attributed to techonological innovation.
- have to do with the fact that ...： 原因在于
	- Many conflicts have to do with the fact that people refuse to listen to each other
